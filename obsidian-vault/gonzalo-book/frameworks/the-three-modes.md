## Definition
Three distinct operating states Gonzalo moves between: Cruise Control (habitual autopilot, low intentionality, coasting on existing systems), Rigid Structure (rule-following without presence, compliance without awareness), and Conscious Presence (deliberate engagement, moment-to-moment awareness, full contact with what is actually happening). The goal is not to eliminate cruise control but to know which mode you are in.
