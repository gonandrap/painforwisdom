## Definition
Three categories of resistance encountered in growth work. Growth friction is productive difficulty that builds capacity — it is the friction that makes you stronger. System friction is structural resistance caused by poor design, missing tools, or misaligned environment — it wastes energy without building anything. Pattern friction is self-generated resistance that comes from internal conflict, identity tension, or old stories running against new commitments — it is the most subtle and the most expensive.
