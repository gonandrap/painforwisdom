# Testing vs. Assuming

**First appeared:** 2026-02-25
**Last updated:** 2026-02-25
**Entry count:** 1

## Core tension
Why do people treat their untested beliefs about their own capacity as facts, and what does it cost them?

## Key insight so far
There is a hard gap between what you believe you can do and what reality will confirm or deny. Most people never cross that gap — they make decisions about their limits from inside the belief, never letting evidence weigh in. The hypothesis stays a hypothesis, dressed up as wisdom.
