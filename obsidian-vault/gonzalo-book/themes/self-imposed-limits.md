# Self-Imposed Limits

**First appeared:** 2026-02-25
**Last updated:** 2026-02-25

## Core tension
Is your "maximum" a fact about your body or a story your mind accepted without evidence?

## Key insight so far
People routinely install ceilings on their own capacity before ever testing them — treating assumption as data and calling it smart self-regulation. The ceiling feels real because it was never challenged, not because it was confirmed.
